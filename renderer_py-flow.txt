    Calls handleFairValueProject() → sends start-py-fairValue to the backend.

    Renderer:
    window.api.receive('py-fairValue-complete', handleFairValueComplete);

    UI Update:
    handleFairValueComplete() calls appState.fetchAndHandlePortData() to refresh data.
